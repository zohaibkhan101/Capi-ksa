About Us

Blending Tradition with Innovation

CAPI Global Company is a visionary Saudi Arabian firm specializing in integrating the Kingdom’s rich architectural heritage with modern engineering innovation. Established in alignment with Saudi Vision 2030, we are dedicated to preserving and reviving traditional regional styles while creating timeless, functional structures.

CAPI is a company dedicated to blending the past and present in architectural design. In line with the 2030 vision, we aim to reflect Saudi Arabia's historical richness by integrating Najdi, Alahsa, and Alhejaz styles.

Our goal is to become a leader in this field by creating unique trademark finishes that combine classic and modern elements. We will establish an engineering office to initiate local standards and collaborate with other systems for optimal results. CAPI is committed to leaving a lasting impact on the architectural industry.
